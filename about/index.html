More About Orange Iceberg.

Orange Iceberg is a small web development consultancy focused on transforming our client's ideas to functional web applications.

We specialize in building web applications using Ruby and Ruby on Rails. However depending on a project we often assemble and lead a team of various web professionals, with help from our associates.

Why the name Orange Iceberg?

An iceberg is often over 90% underwater with only tip being visible. Most web applications (and software in general) are like that too. There's a pretty interface that makes about 10% of a web app, but underneath it there's still 90% of the app.

We make web applications. They are like icebergs in some sense. It just made sense.

Who am I?

My name is Slobodan Kovačević. I have been a professional web developer for past decade, more precisely since 1998. Computers have been my love since I was a little kid and I have been messing with them way before I started using them professionally.

I have been freelancing from 1998 until September 2001 when I co-founded Array Studio, a small web development company which at one point had grew up to 6 developers/designers. Over period of 7 years I worked as CEO and lead developer in Array Studio.

In June 2008 I sold my share of Array Studio and went back to freelancing through Orange Iceberg.

If you have a desire to know even more about me then go to my personal site.

What do I do?

Last year or two I have been focusing on creating web apps using Ruby on Rails, but before that I have mainly used PHP for web development. Actually, there’s a What do I do page – so read it for more details.
