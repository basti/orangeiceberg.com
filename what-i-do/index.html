Commercial work

I am a freelance web developer and consultant with over 10 years of experience. I build web applications mainly using Ruby on Rails.

Through consultations with my clients I help them define their requirements and execute it from start to finish. If required I can also help assemble and lead a team of web professionals.

For more information (about my availability, rates, estimates, etc.) or if you’d like to hire me you can contact me.

Open Coffee Club Novi Sad

In 2007 I started organizing a local Open Coffee Club in Novi Sad. Open Coffee Club meetings are informal gatherings for web professionals where we can exchange opinions and talk about latest tech stuff.
